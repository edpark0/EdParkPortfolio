About Me

Hello! My name is Ed. I am currently living and working in Rochester, New York. My area of focus is constructing and improving websites for businesses that seek a stronger digital presence.

I graduated from University at Buffalo with a B.S. in Computer Engineering. In my former role, I was a systems engineer at Xerox for six years. I created and customized Sharepoint websites for clients. In addition, I managed a system for processing Sharepoint data in a database. This year, I transitioned into web development with a desire to help businesses succeed while exercising my passions for designing websites and developing database solutions.

When I am not coding or speaking with clients, I volunteer at the East Rochester Library by troubleshooting technical problems and teaching folks computer skills. I love baking all kinds of scrumptious desserts and sharing them with my friends and fellow toastmasters. I joined Pinnacle Toastmasters club in Pittsford, New York to work on gaining public speaking skills.
